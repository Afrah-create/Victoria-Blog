Artist Statement

My artistic practice explores the expressive potential of painting as a medium for reflecting lived experiences, cultural narratives, and contemporary realities. As a fine art student working within the course unit of Exhibition, my work focuses on using painting and its diverse materials to investigate themes that emerge from everyday life and the social environment around me.

Through the use of paint, texture, color, and layered surfaces, I seek to communicate emotions, ideas, and stories that connect personal experiences with broader community contexts. Painting allows me to experiment with different materials and techniques, enabling the transformation of ordinary subjects into visually engaging compositions that invite reflection and dialogue.

In my practice, materials such as acrylics, oils, mixed media, and various painting tools become essential elements in shaping meaning. The physical process of applying, layering, and manipulating paint becomes a way of constructing visual narratives that reveal traces of movement, memory, and identity.

PAINTING   “BOUND BY SILENCE”

As an artist shaped by the experiences within my community, I have long been aware of the silent suffering many children endure. “Bound by Silence” was created from my personal commitment to addressing hidden forms of violence and using art as a voice for the vulnerable. Stories of fear, mistreatment, and emotional trauma I encountered while growing up motivated me to confront this sensitive issue visually.

The painting centers a frightened child overshadowed by an intimidating hand holding a belt, while chained hands behind him symbolize captivity and ongoing oppression. The dark tones and sharp contrasts reflect the weight and tension of abuse, highlighting the child’s vulnerability and emotional distress. This imagery communicates the reality that many children face but are unable to express.

Through this work, I aim to evoke empathy, awareness, and discomfort—responses necessary for change. My intention is to challenge communities to break the silence around violence, protect children, and commit to meaningful transformation.
